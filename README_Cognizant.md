Cognizant SkillSpring (summary)

Cognizant SkillSpring is a multimodal, AI-native, conversational learning platform designed to redefine learning in the AI era and help businesses cultivate AI-ready talent at scale. SkillSpring personalizes learning, accelerates skill mastery, and embeds learning into the flow of work. Candidates will be enrolled in relevant courses and receive access instructions by email within ~1.5 weeks of the Deep Skilling start.

Recommendations:
- Use SkillSpring courses noted in the handbook.
- Attend bi-weekly SME connect sessions (100% attendance required).
- Complete hands-on exercises and final KBA assessment.
